About

General

This Firefox Web extension is designed to easily get the focused element the way you want. You can have details about the focussed element using the Web console, a border or by highlighting the element.

Either click the icon or press Ctrl+Shift+F (control+Shift+F on Mac) to see where your keyboard focus is.
